Eneagram Logos

Process Summary

This project began with taking an Enneagram personality test, in which I most closely aligned with The Observer. I was then tasked with creating a brand logo representing this personality type. I developed a game show–inspired logo for a fictional show titled The Observer and animated it with darting eyes, drawing from my personal experience of observing the world around me.
