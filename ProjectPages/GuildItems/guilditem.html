Guild Items

Process Summary

This project was my first large 3D modeling project. I was instructed to choose a theme and design a series of items around that theme. My chosen theme was courier gear based off the job of a fantasy themed medieval times courier. I also decided to create a link between my items under the idea that they were all being sold by a courier guild. I created a bag, a cloak, a hip pouch, a decoder, a potion bottle, a scroll, and a magical smoke bomb.
