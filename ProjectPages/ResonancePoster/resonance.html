Resonance Poster

Process Summary

For this poster, I explored the concept of pushing oneself to the limit using the phrase “work to the bone.” Inspired by Russian Constructivism, I designed the layout and incorporated animated elements, including falling bones and a moving eye based on a photograph of my own eye, edited into the poster to create a feeling of unease and being watched. This project allowed me to experiment with a new design style while integrating motion to enhance the viewer experience.
