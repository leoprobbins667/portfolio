PokéCollector App

Process Summary

PokéCollector is an iOS mobile application designed for Apple phones and tablets that allows users to digitally organize and manage their Pokémon card collections. The app enables users to search an external API for existing Pokémon cards and add them to a personalized collection database. Users can also create and store custom cards by drawing their own designs or uploading images. Additional features include interactive quizzes, statistical charts for collection tracking, and video storage, providing tools to support and enhance the user’s overall collecting experience.
