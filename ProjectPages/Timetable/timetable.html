VT Timetable

Process Summary

This project was given to us and we were told to redesign the current timetable of classes for Virginia Tech as the university was looking to create a new one that is more accessible and understandable for students. As a group of three, I and two other designers worked to redesign the layout and design of the timetable, as well as adding new features to improve the website.
