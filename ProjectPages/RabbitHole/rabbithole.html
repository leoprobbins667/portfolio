Rabbit Hole

Process Summary

This project explored the hidden lore of the Subnautica game series, dividing the story into three eras: events before the first game, during the first game, and in the sequel. I researched the narrative and personally illustrated all poster assets, integrating game-inspired visuals with key information to create an engaging and informative display.
